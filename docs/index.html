🔬 The Science Behind It

Symphonic-Joules is built on the acoustic energy density equation, which governs how sound carries energy through space:

w = p² / (2ρc²) + ρv² / 2

Where:
• w = acoustic energy density (J/m³)
• p = sound pressure (Pa)
• ρ = medium density (kg/m³)
• c = speed of sound (m/s)
• v = particle velocity (m/s)

This fundamental equation bridges two domains:

Research Areas

  • Acoustic Energy: How sound waves carry and transform energy through different media
  • Musical Patterns: Relationships between harmonic structures and energy distributions
  • Computational Acoustics: Numerical methods for analyzing sound and energy
  • Signal Processing: Time-frequency analysis of audio signals
  • Physics-Informed Computing: Applying physical principles to audio data analysis

Project Phases

  • Phase 1 (Current): 🏗️ Foundation - Infrastructure and scientific groundwork
  • Phase 2 (Planned): 🔬 Analysis - Core acoustic and energy analysis capabilities
  • Phase 3 (Planned): 📊 Visualization - Intuitive exploration of acoustic energy data
